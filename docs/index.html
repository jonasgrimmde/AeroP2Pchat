How it works

Share an ID, accept the request, start talking.

  1. 1

    Copy your Aero ID

    The app creates an ID when it starts. Send it to the person you want to reach.

  2. 2

    Connect by request

    Your friend pastes the ID, sends a request, and the receiving side accepts before the chat opens.

  3. 3

    Use chat, calls, or screen share

    Messages travel over WebRTC after the PeerJS signaling step, and media controls stay inside the same conversation.

Desktop behavior

Feels native without hiding where things are.

Tray and startup

Start on sign-in, open normally or minimized, and close to tray when you want Aero to stay available.

Light and dark themes

The app stores settings in Electron userData and keeps the interface consistent across Windows and Linux.

Audio controls

Voice isolation, sensitivity, noise reduction, boost, EQ, and remote volume controls are available in settings.
